Developers
Xabber for Web was originally developed by Redsolution — an international software and services company currently based in Estonia. Since then, a number of individuals joined Xabber as developers, testers and translators.
Our goal is to create a stable, reliable, interoperable and user friendly ecosystem for instant messaging that does not rely on proprietary services and data silos. We welcome anyone who believes in open standards and free information interchange to take part in moving Xabber forward.
Follow us on Twitter and Github.
Translators
Xabber is available in multiple languages thanks to many fine people from all over the world. We have created a special page on our website to acknowledge their efforts.
You may join their ranks and help us improve translation quality by contributing your translations to Xabber project
